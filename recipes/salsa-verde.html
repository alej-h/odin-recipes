Salsa Verde Recipe

Description:

This delicious salsa verde is sure to pack a punch and add some spice to your meal!

Made with green tomatillos and other organic ingredients, once you try it you'll be hooked and want to make it again! Best of all, its lower calorie so you can eat this during a cut!

Ingredients:

  • 6 Green Tomatillos
  • 2 Chile Seranos with Seeds
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 White Onion
  • Handful of Cilantro
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Pepper

We've always eye measured the amount of cilantro, salt, and pepper we use. While we don't want it to be a little, we do want to add enough so that it leaves us with flavor, but doesn't overpower other ingredients.

Directions

  1. Start by cutting your tomattilos in half and boiling them
  2. While the tomatillos are boiling, take out your cilantro and cut it into small, thin pieces.
  3. While/After cutting the cilantro, be sure to check on the tomatillos and boil until soft.
  4. After cutting cilantro, cut the white onion then toss everything together in a blender.
  5. After adding to blender, begin to process it and add a bit of salt and pepper while blending.
  6. Turn off blender, grab a small spoon adn taste, adjust salt and pepper to your liking then enjoy!
